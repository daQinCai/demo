Established in 2003, Shui Yu Medical Technology Co.,Ltd is always focusing on health. With the mission "making life healthier" in mind, we have put the concept of health to thousands of families.We are striving to build an all-around ecology culture with our medicines and household medical devices.

With its headquater located in Hangzhou, Shui Yu is collaborating with multiple brands in the medical field to promote the overall sales both online and offline.The brands we are working with include Resmed,Curative, Zhongde etc. Besides, we have our own brands like "Shi Jing" and "Usound".The first and the second word from "Shi Jing" derive from the name of two famous ancient medical experts "Li ShiZhen”and "Zhang ZhongJing" respectively.The Shi Jing Pharmacy is a successful example of combination with the Internet. It not only greatly facilitates the patients and medical staff, but also saves a lot of social resources.

In January 2018, we established the Hangzhou sleep apnea and disorder study institute in the Yangtze River Delta to raise the awareness of both the public and medical staff toward this disease. We have always been studying the way to prevent OSAHS from happening and trying our very best to improve the patients' health.

Shui Yu grasps the scientific and technological trend with the internet, drastically changes the traditional medical field and provides a convenient way for patients to communicate. Combined with AI technology, the "yuntaixin" mobile app has benefitted thousands of pregant women. Its media team "Shi Se Shuo" publicizes with pictures, videos and live presentation to promote health and extend the value of the company in the medical media, which currently ranks third in the field.

Shui Yu will further devote itself to the medical field and bring the scientific and advanced health concept to the public. Its value " Progress, Centripetalism, Goodness" will be fully implemented to make life healthier.
